About Corona Virus and Precautions

Coronavirus what it is?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. Most people who fall sick with COVID-19 will experience mild to moderate symptoms and recover without special treatment

How to Protect Yourself

Wash your
hands frequently

Maintain social
distancing

Avoid touching eyes,
nose and mouth

Wear a mask if they must be around other people. Masks shouldn't be worn by kids younger than 2 years old or anyone who has trouble breathing
As much as possible, keep away from other people and pets in the home
Stay home if you feel unwell.If possible, stay in a bedroom and use a bathroom separate from other people in the home.
Eat healthy diet and maintain healthy mental-health.
Get vaccinated.
